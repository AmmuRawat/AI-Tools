About IP Geolocation
What is IP Geolocation?

IP Geolocation is the process of determining the physical location of an IP address. This information can include:

  • Country, region, and city
  • Latitude and longitude coordinates
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Organization name
  • Timezone
Note: IP Geolocation data is approximate and may not always be accurate.
