About This Tool

Check and analyze your website's backlinks to understand your link profile. Backlinks are important for SEO as they help search engines determine the authority and relevance of your website.

Features:

  • Check all backlinks to your website
  • Filter by DoFollow and NoFollow links
  • Analyze domain authority of linking sites
  • View anchor text distribution
  • Export backlink data
