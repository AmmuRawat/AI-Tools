About This Tool

Analyze the keyword density in your content to optimize for search engines. Keyword density is the percentage of times a keyword appears in your content compared to the total number of words.

Features:

  • Calculate keyword density in your content
  • Exclude common words from analysis
  • Set minimum word length
  • View keyword distribution chart
  • Export analysis results
