About Text to Speech

This tool converts your text into natural-sounding speech. You can customize the voice, speech rate, pitch, and volume to create the perfect audio output.

Features:
  • Multiple voice options
  • Adjustable speech rate
  • Customizable pitch and volume
  • Real-time playback
  • Download audio files
  • Support for multiple languages
How to Use
  1. Enter or paste your text
  2. Select your preferred voice and settings
  3. Click "Speak" to start playback
  4. Use pause/resume/stop controls
  5. Download the audio file if needed
