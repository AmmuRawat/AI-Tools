About This Tool

This tip calculator helps you quickly calculate tips and split bills:

  • Calculate tips based on bill amount and percentage
  • Split bills among multiple people
  • Round up to nearest dollar or custom amount
  • Quick access to common tip percentages
  • Save and view calculation history

How to Use:

  • Enter the bill amount
  • Select tip percentage or use quick tip buttons
  • Enter number of people to split the bill
  • Choose rounding option if needed
  • Click "Calculate" to see results

Note: All calculations are done in real-time. The tool supports both decimal and whole number inputs.
