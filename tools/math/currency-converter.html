About This Tool

This currency converter helps you convert between different currencies with real-time exchange rates:

  • Convert between major world currencies
  • View real-time exchange rates
  • Track rate history and changes
  • Quick access to popular currency pairs
  • Conversion history with recall functionality

How to Use:

  • Enter the amount you want to convert
  • Select the source currency
  • Choose the target currency
  • Click "Convert" to see the result
  • View rate history and popular currencies

Note: Exchange rates are updated periodically and may vary from actual market rates. This tool is for informational purposes only.
