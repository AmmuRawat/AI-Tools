About This Tool

This loan EMI calculator helps you understand your loan payments and plan your finances:

  • Calculate monthly EMI (Equated Monthly Installment)
  • View total interest and total payment amounts
  • Get a detailed payment schedule
  • See payment breakdown in a visual chart
  • Track your calculation history

How to Use:

  • Enter the loan amount
  • Specify the annual interest rate
  • Set the loan term in years or months
  • Choose the loan start date
  • Click "Calculate EMI" to see the results

Note: This calculator provides estimates based on the information you provide. Actual loan terms and conditions may vary. Always consult with your financial institution for exact details.
