About This Tool

This BMI calculator provides detailed information about your body mass index and related health metrics:

  • Calculate your BMI using different units (metric/imperial)
  • Get your BMI category and health risk assessment
  • View your ideal weight range
  • Calculate daily calorie needs
  • Track your calculation history

How to Use:

  • Enter your height (cm, ft, or in)
  • Enter your weight (kg or lbs)
  • Click "Calculate BMI" to see the results
  • View your calculation history on the right

Note: BMI is a simple weight-to-height ratio and may not be accurate for athletes, pregnant women, or the elderly. Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
