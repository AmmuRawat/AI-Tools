About This Tool

This discount calculator helps you determine the final price after applying discounts and taxes:

  • Calculate discount amount and final price
  • Include optional tax calculations
  • Support for multiple currencies
  • Detailed price breakdown
  • Calculation history with recall functionality

How to Use:

  • Enter the original price of the item
  • Specify the discount percentage
  • Optionally add a tax rate
  • Select your preferred currency
  • Click "Calculate" to see the results
